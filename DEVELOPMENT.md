## Release Branching Strategy
- **Pre-release phase**
Each stable iteration is committed to a dedicated alpha branch:
```alpha-stable-#```

- Post-release phase
Each stable iteration is committed to a dedicated beta branch:
```beta-stable-#```

## Commit policy
All commit messages must be **clear, descriptive,** and **sufficiently detailed.**

---

## Restrictions
**Do not:**
- Modify existing dependencies without justification and review
- Label non-functional builds as “stable”
- Alter or rewrite previous stable branches
- Deviate from the guidelines defined in [STACK.md]
